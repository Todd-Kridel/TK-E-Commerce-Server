TK "E-Commerce Server" Application

* Demonstration Videos *

IMPORTANT:

For better video resolution...be sure to maximize the video display frame and maybe also the containing web browser tab; first popping-out the frame into a separate window/tab by using the diagonal up-right arrow button that is located at the upper-right corner of the frame. Also...the current display resolution of the video display frame can be increased by clicking the cog (Settings) button. Otherwise...For possible better resolution and play speed/smoothness...you can download the video either from the maximized video display frame...or by clicking the ".mp4" video file names of the GitHub repository window (which is accessed from the README file link at below) and then clicking the "Download" button that is located at the right side of the information line that indicates the MB size of the desired video file[s]. NOTE: THE DEMO VIDEOS ARE AUTOMATICALLY DOWNLOADED IF THE REPOSITORY IS CLONED; AND THEN YOU CAN PLAY THE VIDEOS FROM THE LOCAL CLONE DIRECTORY.

The 3 videos are in the repository file-set ("0_E-Commerce_Configuration_and_Start-Up.mp4" and "1-2-3_E-COMMERCE_GET_IDS_ALL_AND_BY_ID_AND_DELETE_BY_ID.mp4" and "4_E-COMMERCE_CREATE_ID_AND_UPDATE_BY_ID.mp4") and at Google drives.
